Project History

It all started in the Spring of 2025, when a bunch of suitemates in their sophomore year at Rutgers, realized they spent way to much time in their dorms. But when they went out to go touch some grass, they realized that their campus had very little natural area. Dissatisfied, they knew they had to look farther out if they ever wanted to find a spot to call their own. After much searching, they stumbled upon a park that was only a short walk from their building, so they decided to check it out. The name of this park is Johnson Park, and it will forever be the first park cataloged by the Park Project. After this fateful day, the group made it their mission to discover and document every park and greenspace in the Greater Rutgers area, with a goal of helping students who are looking for a place to escape.
